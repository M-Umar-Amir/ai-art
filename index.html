Questions

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know about working with Epical Studio

What exactly is AI cinematic production?

AI cinematic production uses cutting-edge generative AI tools — guided by a human creative director — to produce imagery and video that matches the visual quality of traditional film production. Think of it as a virtual film set: no cameras, no crews, no expensive locations — but the same cinematic language, lighting, composition, and emotional weight.

Is this just AI-generated content, or is a human actually directing it?

Every piece produced at Epical Studio is directed by a human creative — not just prompted and delivered. That means deliberate decisions around visual storytelling, colour grading, pacing, composition, and brand fit. The AI is our tool, not our director. The difference shows in the output: our work is crafted, not coincidental.

Can I use the images and videos for commercial purposes?

Yes — all Standard and Campaign plans include a full commercial use licence, covering advertising, product packaging, social media, websites, and print. Starter plan covers personal and social use. If you need a broader licence (broadcast, outdoor, OOH), just ask and we'll quote accordingly.

How does the ordering and production process work?

You share your creative brief — mood references, brand colours, subject, and intent. We confirm the scope and timeline, then begin production. You receive a first draft for review, share feedback, and we refine until it's right. Final files are delivered at full resolution, ready to publish. Most projects move from brief to delivery in 24–48 hours.

What if I'm not satisfied with the result?

Every plan includes revision rounds, and we genuinely don't consider a project closed until you're happy. For clients who prefer extra security, ordering via Upwork gives full milestone-based payment protection and formal dispute resolution — you don't pay until you approve the work.

Can you match a specific visual style or replicate a reference?

Absolutely. Send us any reference — a film still, a mood board, a competitor's ad, or even a rough sketch. Our creative director studies the reference and interprets it into original work that captures the feel without copying it. Style-matched orders are one of our most popular request types.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes. We work with clients worldwide. International orders are handled securely through Upwork with full payment protection, or via invoice for established clients. Deliverables are digital and timezone-independent — we've delivered campaigns to clients across the Middle East, UK, and North America.
